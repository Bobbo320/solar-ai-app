📊 What Solar Score Means:

🔥 85–100% → Excellent solar
• Clear skies, strong generation
• Battery likely fills early
• High export potential

👍 65–85% → Good solar
• Partial cloud but solid production
• Battery usually fills
• Some export expected

⚖️ 45–65% → Moderate solar
• Mixed or overcast conditions
• Battery may not fully charge

⚠️ <45% → Low solar
• Weak generation (cloud/rain)
• Prioritise higher SOC to avoid grid import
